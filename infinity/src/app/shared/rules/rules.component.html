Description

This game is a remaster of the 1972 pong, the original concept of Pong is a two-dimensional sports game that simulate table tennis. Two players controls an in-game white paddle on a black background, while a ball is represented by a small white square, moves through the screen and bounces off the top and bottom edges. The player moves his paddle by sliding it vertically using mouse or pad. Players use the paddle to hit a ball back and forth. The speed of the ball increases at every hit of a player. The ball's rebound angle changes depending where it hits the paddle. The goal for the players : be the first to reach 11 points.
In addition, we have a ranking system accompanied by a matchmaking to delight our most competitive players.
